About

Cliff is a working Musician from Ohio, currently creating and studying music full time in Miami.

History

Cliff was born in Dayton, Ohio in 2004. He moved around many places during his life there. At age 16, he moved to Florida with his family. Upon moving out and beginning school at the University of Miami's Frost School of Music, he began to work part time as a musician. In March of 2024, Cliff married his highschool sweetheart, Morgan.

Music Career

From Rock n' Roll to Jazz, Cliff Dobson is a chameleon when it comes to style. He currently plays in multiple bands, including Puddley, his soul, funk, and R&B act, as well as Taylor Davis & the Outsiders, the classic rock powerhouse. He has original music out with 4 different artists, and often contributes drum tracks to other artists for hire. Some of his best collaborations have been with Therum on their album "Two Sunken Sons", and Nicole Acosta's "No More."
